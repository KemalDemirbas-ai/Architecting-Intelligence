## 📌 Overview
**Rhythm-Sequence-AI** is a custom-built simulation of Spotify's background 'Smart Radio / Autoplay' algorithm. Unlike classic recommendation systems, it doesn't just look at a single song; it analyzes the **energy and emotional flow over time of a 5-song sequence** chosen by the user. 

Using Deep Learning, it understands where this musical "Vibe" is heading and **predicts the 6th song in the sequence with pinpoint accuracy.**

## 🧠 How it Works

The system treats music not just as an audio file, but as a mathematical "DNA Vector".

1. **Audio DNA:** 9 different audio features (such as `danceability, energy, loudness, speechiness, acousticness, instrumentalness, liveness, valence, tempo`) are extracted from the selected 5 songs.
2. **Sequential Deep Learning (LSTM):** Instead of standard neural networks, the **Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM)** architecture, capable of understanding time-series data, is utilized. The model analyzes this 5-song arrangement and predicts the expected "DNA Vector" of the 6th song.
3. **Euclidean Distance:** This ideal/imaginary audio DNA generated by the LSTM is scanned against our massive 10,000-song dataset and converted into a real song using the closest vector match (Euclidean Metric).

### 🛠️ How to Use
1. Open the Hugging Face Space interface.
2. Select **5 songs** from the dropdown menu to create a specific "Vibe" (energy flow).
3. Click the `Predict Next Track DNA 🚀` button.
4. Review the 6th song recommended by the AI DJ, along with its predicted Energy, Danceability, and Happiness (Valence) scores.
